PC Specs

Unfortunately the biggest barrier of entry for new Sim-Racers is their PC specs. Fortunatly we can check your PC specs and confirm which simulators can run on your hardware.

The minimum required PC specifications for iRacing are significantly lower than many other games on the market. Many average desktop computers can handle iRacing with ease. Many midrange laptop offerings also are capable of running iRacing at an acceptable framerate.

The reccomended minimum specs for iRacing are as follows:

  • Windows 8.1 64-Bit, Windows 10 64-Bit
  • 4 core CPU or better, Examples (but not limited to): AMD FX-6300, Intel Core i5-4430, Intel Core i5-2320, AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • 8 GB of Ram
  • A gaming graphics card with at least 2GB of DEDICATED memory - Some examples (but not limited to): Nvidia GeForce GTX 660 @ 2 GB / GTX 1050 or ATI Radeon HD 7850 @ 2GB / AMD RX 550 or better
  • Atleast 10GB of free disk space

What if my PC isn't powerful enough?

If you meet MOST of the requirements or come fairly close in almost every catagory, You will most likely still be able to run the sim. I would reccomend purchasing a one month membership to test the games functionality.

If you don't manage to meet the required specs, the best thing you can do is research as many options as possible to avoid overspending. If your computer does not meet ANY of the specs, your only option is to purchase a more powerful machine. However, for slightly more powerful machines it is possible to upgrade to the requried specs. Many modern desktops will meet the requirements for the operating system, processor and disk space. Ram and Graphics Cards can be easily upgraded by a repuatble PC repair store or at home. I would reccomend doing as much research as possible before modifying your computer.
