Choosing a wheel

Picking a steering wheel can be tough. The wheel is one of the most important parts of the sim rig, it is the tool you use to interact with the sim. Buying a wheel is the first step to getting started in sim racing. The easiest way to choose a wheel is to pick the best wheel you can afford. Every single force-feedback steering wheel on the market is adiquite for a beginner. Higher quality wheels are simply a luxury. Unlike real-world racing, in sim racing equipment does not make you a faster driver.

Next Steps

Assuming your PC meets the required specs, a steering wheel is the only thing you need to get started. At this point you can start your sim racing career. Every other piece of sim racing equipment is optional. I would recommend getting some races under your belt before considering upgrading your rig.
